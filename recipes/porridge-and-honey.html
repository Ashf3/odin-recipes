Porridge & Honey!

This is a decription of porridge and honey. There are lots of ingredietns needed to make it taste good. Belwo you will find a list of them.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Honey

Instructions

  1. Add the oats into a microwave safe bowl - give them a little pat
  2. Add milks so that the oats are just about covered, stir a little
  3. Microwave on high for 1 min 30 seconds
  4. Remove from microwave and stir in the honey
  5. Microwave for another 30 seconds or minute depending on preference of consistency
  6. Stir one final time, and enjoy!
