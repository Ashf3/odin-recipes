Cheesecake!

This is a decription of a Cheesecake. There are lots of ingredietns needed to make it taste good. Belwo you will find a list of them.

Ingredients

  • Milk Chocolate Biscuits
  • Butter
  • Cream Cheese
  • Icing Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Double Cream
  • Malteasers

Instructions

  1. Crush the biscuits into tiny pieces
  2. Melt the butter over a low heat and the combine with biscuits
  3. Combine the cream Cheese with icing sugar and vanilla extract. Mix well
  4. Whisk the double cream until soft peaks form
  5. MIx in the broken malteaser pieces
  6. Spoon bicuits onto bottomn of tin/jar and top with the mixture
  7. Let sit for a few hours, then enjoy!
