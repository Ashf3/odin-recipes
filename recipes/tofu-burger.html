Tofu Burger!

This is a decription of a Tofu Burger. There are lots of ingredietns needed to make it taste good. Belwo you will find a list of them.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Soy Souce
  • Siracha Sauce
  • Maple Syrup
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Mayonaisse
  • Burger Stack

Instructions

  1. Mix together soy sauce, siracha, maple syrup, and olive oild to create a marinade
  2. Cover tofu with marinade and let sit for 20 mins on both side
  3. Meanwhle combine siracha, lemon juice, and mayo to make a sauce.
  4. Once marinated, grill tofu for 8 mins on each side
  5. Build burger with tofu, mayo based sauce, and rest of the burger stack
  6. Serve with sweet potatoe fries and enjoy!
